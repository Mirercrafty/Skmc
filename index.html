NEWS: SALES OF IJMB & MATRICULATION, 2023/2024 ACADEMIC SESSION.


This Is to Inform The general public that Kebbi State University of Science and Technology, Aliero wishes to announce the commencement of sales of 1 Year Interim Joint Matriculation Board (IJMB) and MATRICULATION Admission for 2023/2024 Academic Session with effect from Monday 15th May,2023.Applicants seeking for Admission should visit any of the following Banks Eco Bank Plc Aliero (B) Eco Bank Plc, Birnin Kebbi. Applicants are to obtain both IJMB AND MATRICULATION Form at the rate of Five Thousand Naira (N 5,000) Only.Applicants Must have Credit in English Language and 4 Basic Science.
Closing Date for sales and submission of filled Forms will be on Friday 30th June, 2023. FOR IJMB CANDIDATE Students that Passed IJMB within A Range of 7 -16 Points will be considered for Direct Entry Admission (200 Level Students that Passed IJMB would be considered first for Direct Admission @KSUSTA FOR MATRICULATION CANDIDATES Student that scored the required UTME points would be considered first by the University for Admission For More Details, contact the school’s Secretary On 08036477068.
