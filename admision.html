SCHEDULE FEES


The registration Fees for PG Programme 2022/2023 academic session is given below:

(A) Registration Fee Per Session Per Student for Nigerian:
1. Ph.D. and M.Phil. Programme:         ₦130,000.00
2. M.Sc. Programme:                          ₦110,000.00
3. PGD Programme:                            ₦90,000.00.

(B) Registration Fee Per Session Per Student for Foreigner:
1. Ph.D. and M.Phil. Programme:         ₦170,000.00
2. M.Sc. Programme:                          ₦150,000.00
3. PGD Programme:                            ₦130,000.

(C) Students who wish to be accommodated in the University hostel are to pay additional ₦60,000 for a room per session.
